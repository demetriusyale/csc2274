What I Make

I create long form gaming videos detailing the journey of me taking on specific very difficult challenges, and documenting everything I have to do to complete that challenge. I do things in games that most people can't, leveraging my unhealthy addiction to screens to spend hundreds of hours in a video game for each video. This lets me curate which parts of the challenge I want to show to make the most entertaining video possible, while showing people what the journey is like, since many cant spend as much time playing a game as I will.

Choosing these long challenges let's me spend time getting to know the game, and it's community. Many videos about games online only attempt to be surface level glances of what a game offers, while mine tend to show more intricate parts of the game, since I spend so much more time on them than other creators.

This may lead to less video output overall for me, but helps me feel some pride in the fact that I was able to accomplish something that most people won't, even if it's almost entirely because nobody should spend that much time playing a game.
